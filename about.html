Certified Native American and woman-owned. V¢T Technologies is Native-founded and Native-led, with a mission centered on serving underserved communities and building equitable access to technology.

01

Our Mission

Small businesses deserve the same quality of security that protects the largest organizations — at a price that doesn't put them out of business.

V¢T Technologies LLC was founded on the belief that small businesses — the backbone of every community — shouldn't have to choose between staying secure and staying profitable. Big managed service providers are built for enterprise clients. Small businesses get leftover attention, slow response times, and contracts designed for companies ten times their size.

We built something different. Owner-operated, locally rooted in Coon Rapids, Minnesota, and staffed by people with real enterprise-level credentials, V¢T delivers the same quality of protection as the largest MSPs — with the personal accountability only a small firm can offer.

As a Native American and woman-owned business, we are especially committed to serving minority-owned businesses, tribal enterprises, and underrepresented communities who have historically had the least access to quality IT services.
